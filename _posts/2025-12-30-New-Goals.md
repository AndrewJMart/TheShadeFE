# Concluding 2025

Throughout 2025 one common pattern I noticed within myself was an obsession with the future. Chasing my next learning objective, trying to maximize each day, and never taking in the moment. To combat this, I want to take a second to reflect on the current year. For one, I am blessed to live the life I do. I am in good health, I have "great" friends, and I have an amazing partner. Without your tribe you are nothing, so appreciate and take care of your people. I wasn't sure what this year would look like and looking back it resembles a rorschach inkblot... lots of stuff happened and a lot of it is up to interpretation. Life is all about perspective and I am choosing to have the perspective of positivity. 

# So What About 2026?

Reflecting on this past year provided clarity for 2026. My goal is to simply have no goals. Meaning, I want to treat each day as an independent adventure as opposed to stringing days to accomplish goals. My life at the moment is too zoomed out, taking the time to smell the roses is essential to live a fulfilling life. 

In conclusion, I am letting my own imagination drive what 2026 will represent.
